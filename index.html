Instructions

To check multiple checkboxes between two checked checkboxes using the Shift key:

  1. Click the first checkbox in the range you want to select.
  2. Hold down the Shift key on your keyboard. Click the second checkbox in the range you want to select.
  3. All the checkboxes between the first and second checkboxes will be checked automatically.
